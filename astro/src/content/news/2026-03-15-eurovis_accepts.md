We've had two papers accepted to the EuroVis conference. The first is a paper led by Derya on her collaboration with [Östergötlands Stadsmission](https://stadsmissionenost.se/), designing data physicalization workshops for teens: [Thinking Inside the Box: Considerations for Putting Data Physicalization Workshops in a Box](https://visidlab.github.io/publications/2026_eurovis_workshop/). The second is a paper led by Miriah on traceability for supporting transparency of visualization research practices: [Reflections on Traceability for Visualization Research](https://visidlab.github.io/publications/2026_eurovis_traceability/). See you in Nottingham in June!
