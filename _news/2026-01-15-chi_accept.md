We've had a paper accepted to the ACM CHI conference. It is a paper that Miriah worked on about articulating the values that undergird visualization tools, guidelines, and pedagogy in collaboration with colleagues at the University of Toronto, University of Zürich, and University of Bergen: [A Critical Reflection on the Values and Assumptions in Data Visualization](https://visidlab.github.io/publications/2026_chi_values/).
